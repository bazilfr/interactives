Quick Financial Terms

  • Stocks: A fraction of ownership in a company.
  • Cryptocurrency: Decentralized digital currency like Bitcoin.
  • Bonds: Loans to governments or corporations with fixed interest.
  • CD (Certificate of Deposit): Savings account with fixed interest for a set time.
